ArQa — Customer Care
Return & Exchange Policy
Everything you need to know
⏰
Time Frame

Returns and exchanges are accepted within 7 days from the delivery date. Requests submitted after this window will not be processed.

✅
Accepted Conditions
  • Item must be unused and in original condition
  • Original packaging must be intact
  • Tags must still be attached
  • Item must not be damaged by the customer
❌
Non-Returnable Items
  • Customized or personalized items
  • Items purchased on final sale
  • Earrings (for hygiene reasons)
💰
Refund Process

Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days after we receive the item. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the item was defective or incorrect.

📦
Shipping

For exchanges, ArQa covers the shipping cost of the replacement item. For returns due to customer preference, the customer covers return shipping.
